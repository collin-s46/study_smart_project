About Study Smart

Study Smart is a web app built by students, for students — designed to simplify the way we manage our school life. It combines a personalized task checklist with a clean event calendar to help you stay on top of assignments, tests, and important dates. Each task you add is scored by priority based on its difficulty, due date, and importance, so you always know what to tackle first. Study Smart isn’t just about getting organized — it’s about building better habits, reducing stress, and making school feel a little less overwhelming. We believe productivity tools should be simple, intuitive, and actually helpful. That’s what Study Smart is here for.
